Real Madrid

This legendary team, featuring players like Alfredo Di Stéfano, Ferenc Puskás, and Paco Gento, is widely lauded. Real Madrid is famous for its "Galácticos" policy, where they sign top global stars like Ronaldo, Zinedine Zidane, and David Beckham, particularly during Florentino Pérez's presidency.

During Bernabéu's presidency, many of Real Madrid's most legendary names played for the club, including Di Stéfano, Ferenc Puskás, Francisco Gento, Héctor Rial, Raymond Kopa, José Santamaría, Miguel Muñoz, Amancio and Santillana.Real Madrid leads in head-to-head results in official competitive matches with 105 wins to Barcelona's 100 with 52 draws as of the match played on 21 April 2024. Along with Athletic Bilbao, they are the only clubs in La Liga to have never been relegated.Cristiano Ronaldo is Real Madrid's all-time leading goalscorer with 450 goals in total.
